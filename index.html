Welcome!

My name is Estella and I am born and raised in Oregon. As someone who's lived here their whole life, trust me, I know the spots! Here you will find breathtaking locations in Oregon I have personally visited, photographed, summarized and provided a link to the location. I hope this website will help you on your travels.

Portland Japanese Garden

The Portland Japanese Garden is one of my favorite spots to visit. It is famously renowned as the best Japanese Garden in the world outside of Japan. The scenery here is unforgettable and relaxing. They also have a traditional dining area that looks over a beautifully gardened hill, as well as a high quality gift shop.

Ape Caves

The Oregon Ape Caves are a unique and adventurous destination. These lava tubes are one of the longest in North America, offering an underground journey through volcanic history. The caves provide both easy and challenging routes, making them perfect for all explorers. The cool, dark atmosphere creates an exciting yet peaceful experience.

Seal Rock

Seal Rock on the Oregon Coast is a stunning seaside location. It features large rock formations along the shoreline, often surrounded by resting seals and sea birds. The views of the Pacific Ocean are breathtaking, especially during sunset. The tide pools here are full of fascinating marine life, and there are amazing trails to explore in the area.

Peninsula Park Rose Garden

The Peninsula Park Rose Garden is a hidden gem in Portland. This historic garden is home to thousands of colorful rose bushes arranged in elegant patterns. The park's fountain and pavilion add to its charm. It's a perfect place for a relaxing stroll or a quiet afternoon surrounded by nature's beauty.

Multnomah Falls

Multnomah Falls is one of Oregon's most iconic natural landmarks. The waterfall cascades down in two tiers, creating a stunning sight year-round. A scenic footbridge allows visitors to get close to the falls. The surrounding forest and misty atmosphere provide beautiful hikes and an unforgettable experience.

Lincoln City Beach

Lincoln City Beach is a beautiful stretch of Oregon coastline. The wide sandy beach is perfect for long walks, kite flying, or beachcombing. This location is also known for its hidden glass floats left by local artists. The fresh ocean breeze and calming waves make it an ideal place to relax and enjoy nature.
